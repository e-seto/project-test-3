Descriptive Analysis

Descriptive analysis answers "What happened?" by summarizing the raw data. Below we explore the distribution of fraud across transaction categories and time of day to understand the basic patterns in our dataset of 3.48 million transactions.

Fraud Rate by Transaction Category

Online shopping (shopping_net) and miscellaneous online transactions (misc_net) have the highest fraud rates at 1.74% and 1.52% respectively. This makes sense — online transactions lack physical card verification, making them easier targets for fraudsters. In-person categories like home and kids/pets have rates below 0.15%.

Fraud Rate by Hour of Day

Fraud spikes dramatically during late-night hours (10 PM – 3 AM), peaking at 2.56% at 11 PM. During normal business hours (4 AM – 9 PM), fraud rates hover around 0.10–0.15%. This pattern suggests fraudsters operate when cardholders are asleep and less likely to notice unauthorized charges.
